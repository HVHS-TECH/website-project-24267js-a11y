COCO CHANEL

Chanel is one of the most iconic luxery fashion houses in the world, founded in 1910 by Gabrielle Coco Chanel in Paris at a time when women's fashion was dominated by restrictive corsets and elaborate, impractical designs. Chanel revolutionized style by introducing a radically simpler, more comfortable aesthetic inspired by menswear and everyday practicality, popularizing garments like jersey dresses, tailored suits, and the concept of casual elegence. In the 1920's, the brand cemented its legacy with creations such as the little black dress and the launch of Chanel No.5, one of the most famous fragrences ever made, which helped define modern femininity.
