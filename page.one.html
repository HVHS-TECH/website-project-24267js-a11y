COCO CHANEL

Chanel is a renowned french luxery fashion house founded in 1910 by Gabrielle "Coco" Chanel, specializing haute couture, ready-to-wear, handbags, fragrences and cosmetics. Known for timeless elegence, the brand revolutionized women's fashion by ditching corset's for comfort, stylish clothing, establishing iconic staples like The Little Black Dress (worn by Audrey Hepburn from The Breakfast Club), tweed suits amd the No.5 perfume.
